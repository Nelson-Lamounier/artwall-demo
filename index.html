About Us

Experience

We have worked hard to secure the trust of all our clients with 90% of our work delivered through repeat business and strong relationships with our clients. Our client portfolio features a diverse range of commercial projects.

Digital

We can create large bespoke paintings using digital graphic files such as photos to create large artworks, murals, or text signage which can be printed on plaster, sheetrock, glass, steel, brick, concrete, vinyl, and wood.

New Technology

Advanced painting technology to improve any space with eye-catching promotions and branding messages. Specially designed for long-term indoor and outdoor decoration, we can create a dedicated feature wall or using your whole office as a blank canvas, create any artwork you desire. This can translate into a real business opportunity to strengthen your business brand with minimal time and effort.

Fast

We can help you deliver your project in a speedy way, by eliminating the burden of using existing methods such as digital wallpaper. These methods involve designing, handling and application, as well as requiring qualified paperhangers, all of which are extremely time consuming.

Vegan Ink

We can print up to 16 square metres per hour in full colour CMYK spectrum with maximum resolution of up to 900 X 1200 dpi. Using UV inks, and vegan ink, free from solvents and thinners. With Vegan ink, the main benefit is that it’s vegan-friendly and ideal to keep your business eco-friendly.

Design

We can print with height vertically up to 3,50 m, with a speed up to 8,00 m² printing per hour. If quality is at the heart of your design, we can produce precise quality results with speed at 2,0-4,0 m² per hour. All done using high-quality industrial grade components from leading manufacturers in the EU and operate via WiFi.
